Appointment System

Notice to minors scheduling a Behind-the-Wheel drive test appointment:

Minors must have had an instruction permit for at least 6 months to be eligible for a Behind-the-Wheel drive test. This appointment system will check eligibility based on the first available appointment date. Please call 1-800-777-0133 to book a specific date that is past the 6 months requirement.
